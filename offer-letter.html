Offer Letter Process

Thank you for showing interest in our Micro IT Internship program. Once you apply through our official application link and successfully join our community, your internship journey will officially begin.

After your onboarding in our communication platform, you will receive detailed tasks and mentorship support from our internal team.

Once your participation is confirmed in the community and your initial engagement is validated, you will receive an official offer letter from our organization. This serves as confirmation of your internship role and outlines the learning goals.

The offer letter will also include start and expected end dates, internship domain, and coordinator contact details.

Please ensure you actively communicate and complete your initial setup steps to receive this formal document within a few working days.
