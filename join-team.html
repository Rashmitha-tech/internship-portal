Join the Internal Team

Interns who exhibit excellent commitment, creativity, and problem-solving skills are considered for a chance to join our Micro IT internal team.

Selection is based on project performance, consistency, and contributions in collaborative tasks and forums.

Selected interns will be offered part-time contributor roles and get the chance to mentor other new interns.

This opportunity helps in building leadership qualities and deeper industry experience.

If you're interested, please fill out the "Join Our Team" form and attend the selection interviews organized monthly.
