Certificate Verification

As part of your internship journey at Micro IT, successful completion of all assigned tasks is crucial to earn your internship certificate.

Each intern must actively participate in weekly assignments, complete milestone projects, and engage in knowledge-sharing sessions.

Once your mentors verify that you've completed all required deliverables, your profile is submitted for final review.

The verification team checks your task status and overall performance consistency before issuing the official certificate.

Certified interns will receive a digital certificate via email, which can be used in resumes, college credits, and portfolio showcases.
